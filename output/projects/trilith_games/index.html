Trilith Games - identification

According to wiki sources Trilith is a structure built from two vertical stones (posts) and third one set horizontally across the top of them (lintel). If You cannot imagine it, check what Stonehenge is made of.

Trilith Games is small development team, consists of three people, each with individual set of skills.

The main goal of the logo design was to not literally visualize trilith shape. I decided to take as a base another shape with hidden symbol/meaning of number three behind it - a triangle.

Fonts used in desing: Bebas Neue Regular and Arial Bold.

Technologies used: Illustrator
