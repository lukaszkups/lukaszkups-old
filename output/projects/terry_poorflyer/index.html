Terry Poorflyer - game & level design, tests

Terry Poorflyer is a family fan project where my task was to develop all neccessary sprites(character, environment, monsters, animation frames), create levels(Tiled) and test it.

This is a family game platformer, created with Flash technology.

Style and mechanics of the game refers to old good times of TV games from the 8-bit NES or Pegasus console type. Inspired by games such as Mario(NES), Rick Dangerous(Commodore 64) or Indiana Jones(NES).

Player controls a Terry Poorflyer - a pilot who - as his name suggests itself - is pretty bad flyer and accidentaly crashes on the island at the middle of the ocean.

The main task is to reach out to the local airport, which unfortunately is quite far - the player will have to go through 10 different levels (beach, desert, jungle or caves) gathering on the occasion scattered coins here and there (their fate is shrouded in mystery, but there is a legend about that someone collect them all and received a surprise at the end of the game :)) or water bottles which restors stamina of our hero.

Technologies used: Photoshop, Illustrator, Graphics Gale, Tiled, Flash
